A Brief Description of Each Character

  • Sarah: A talented and sensitive violinist who finds her identity and peace in music. She represents the fragile beauty of life that was extinguished by the Holocaust.

  • Samuel: Sarah's younger brother, a kind and quiet boy who finds solace and wisdom in books. He symbolizes the innocence and intellectual curiosity that the war crushed.

  • Elara: A practical, resilient, and resourceful young woman with a talent for sewing. She represents the strength and determination required to survive, and the hope that can be found even in the darkest of times.

  • David: A strong and loyal blacksmith's apprentice. He is the embodiment of steadfastness and commitment, symbolizing the enduring power of friendship and love.
